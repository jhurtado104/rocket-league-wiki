Vehicles

At the launch of Rocket League there were a total of eleven cars to choose from. This does not include console exclusives. Below you can click and view each car in there respective team (orange and blue team) and can see how they differ from one another while in game. Among the early Rocket League Community the hitboxes of each car were dicussed and many speculated that some cars were similar to each other in the way they feel on the pitch and their interactions with other vehicles. As of today we now know that there are six types of hitboxes or body types in the game which include Octane, Dominus, Plank, Breakout, Hybrid, and Merc. Each body type has its pros and cons but at the end of the day it all depends on the player and what they decided to master.
